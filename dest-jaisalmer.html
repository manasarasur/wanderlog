Jaisalmer (Golden City) Travel Guide

Quick Travel Facts

  • Best Time to Visit: November to February
  • Stay Duration: 2-3 Days
  • Local Transport: Rented Scooter / Auto
  • Difficulty: Easy (Desert climate)

Jaisalmer, known as the "Golden City" of Rajasthan, stands in the heart of the Thar Desert. Constructed out of yellow sandstone, the entire city glows under the desert sun. It is dominated by the colossal Jaisalmer Fort, a living fort where a quarter of the town's population still resides inside the walls.

How to Reach Jaisalmer

By Train: Jaisalmer Railway Station (JSM) is well-connected to major junctions like Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Delhi via overnight trains, including the *Jaisalmer Express*.

By Flight: Jaisalmer Airport operates seasonal flights to Delhi, Mumbai, and Jaipur. Alternatively, Jodhpur airport is located 280 km away.

Recommended Desert Experiences

  • The Living Fort: Walk the winding stone corridors of Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Qila). Explore Jain temples and local sandstone *havelis* (merchant houses).
  • Sam Sand Dunes Camping: Book a night in a Swiss tent camp at the Sam Dunes. Take a camel safari or jeep tour over the shifting sand crests at sunset.
  • Gadsisar Lake: A scenic, rainwater conservation lake constructed in 1367, surrounded by stone shrines and arched gateways.
